🔄 Pro vs Flash Trade-offs

✓ Choose PRO when:

  • Complex reasoning matters
  • High accuracy critical (medical, legal)
  • Time is not constrained
  • Batch processing
  • Budget flexible

⚡ Choose FLASH when:

  • Real-time apps (chat, etc)
  • Cost optimization needed
  • 55% accuracy is acceptable
  • High throughput required
  • Latency <1 second needed

Performance Gap: Pro = 58-60% | Flash = 52-55% | Difference = 5-6%
